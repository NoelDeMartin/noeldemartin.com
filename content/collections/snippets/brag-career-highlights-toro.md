**Toro** was the first company I joined, and it was all the way in Taiwan!

We did open an office in Barcelona after 5 months, but this gave me the opportunity to experience an international workplace and get way outside of my bubble.

This was probably the most purely technical role I've had: I learned about developing native mobile applications, dealt with assembly language, debugged a proprietary virtual machine, and even implemented a UI rendering engine from scratch. My interests have changed a lot since then, but it definitely was a valuable experience!

Ultimately, I decided to leave because I had caught the entrepreneurship bug.
