In contrast with the rest of technologies here, I wasn't immediately enamored of TypeScript when I first tried it. But it's been growing on me, and as I got more proficient with it, I started missing it when it's gone.

Luckily, JavaScript can really run anywhere, and I could just write TypeScript for the rest of my life. Alas, it's not Laravel, and sometimes I would agree it's too verbose and convoluted.
