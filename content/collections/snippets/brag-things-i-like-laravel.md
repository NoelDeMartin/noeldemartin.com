**[Laravel](https://laravel.com)** is the best framework ever, in any language, everywhere. Not everyone is going to agree with that, specially not the people who think that "PHP is Dead". But what can I say, I have tried countless of frameworks, and there isn't any that has captured my heart quite like Laravel.

The developer experience, the design patterns, the architecture, and the cohesive ecosystem are the best I've seen. In fact, I'm always trying to incorporate ideas from Laravel in other languages and projects. If I could build everything with Laravel, I would!
