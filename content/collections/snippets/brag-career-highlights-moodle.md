If you don't know **[Moodle](https://moodle.org)**, you should know that it's [the most used LMS for schools and higher education](https://stats.moodle.org/); and one of the most used Open Source projects in the world.

Working at Moodle I had the opportunity to make software used by millions of people. In particular, I worked in the Mobile Apps team, which had the unique challenge of having to support a very complex platform in low-end devices. And all of that with full offline capabilities.

Other than the tech, Moodle is a company whose values and ethos are strongly aligned with mine, and it was a joy to spend 5 years working there. Still, all good things come to an end. If you're curious about my decision to leave, I wrote about it in my blog: <a href="/blog/the-end-of-the-chapter" target="_blank">The End of The Chapter</a>.
